Industry Overview

From the expressways transforming the nation's connectivity to the metro corridors reshaping urban mobility, India's Building & Road Infrastructure sector is laying the foundation for a modern, connected, and future-ready economy. With over 65,000 km of highways under development, 15+ metro rail networks expanding, and the National Infrastructure Pipeline driving â‚¹111 lakh crore of investments, India is building one of the world's fastest-growing infrastructure ecosystems.

Market Highlight & Outlook - Buildings

Real Estate Growth

  • Real estate sector expected to reach US$ 1 trillion by 2030, up from US$ 200 billion in 2021
  • Market estimated to increase at a CAGR of 19.5% during 2017-2028
  • Expected to reach US$ 650 billion by 2025, representing 13% of India's GDP

  • ICRA expects new project launches across top seven cities to rise by 6-9% in FY26, reaching 620-640 million square feet

Government Support & Investments

1. Green Building Movement

  • India has 6,548 registered green building projects, among top three countries with green building footprint
  • Over 7,000 IGBC-certified projects covering 1,370 million square feet
  • Third-largest market for LEED globally
  • Green building sector expected to reach US$ 39 billion by 2025
  • Offers 30-50% energy savings, 20-30% water savings

2. Union Budget 2025-26

  • Nil tax on two self-occupied properties (earlier one)
  • TDS threshold on rent raised from Rs. 2.4 lakh to Rs. 6 lakh

  • GST rates reduced to 1% for affordable housing under construction

  • 25-30% penetration projected in office segment by 2030
  • At least one new REIT expected annually over next 3-5 years

4. Land Acquisition Bill

  • Registration Bill 2025 seeks to digitize land and property records
  • Enable online, paperless registration with Aadhaar verification
  • Boost transparency and curb fraud
  • Make remote real estate transactions easier for NRIs and domestic buyers

  • Launched October 2021, enabled evaluation of 293 infrastructure projects worth Rs. 13.59 lakh crore by July 2025

  • March 2025: Cabinet approved 29.219 km 6-lane greenfield highway from JNPA Port to Chowk at Rs. 4,500.62 crore

6. Bhoomi Rashi

  • Portal accelerates publication of notifications for land acquisition

  • September 2025: Delhi received Rs. 803 crore under CRIF for 140+ road and flyover projects

7. Bharatmala Pariyojana Project

  • As of August 2025: 35 Multimodal Logistics Parks being developed with Rs. 50,000 crore outlay

Industry Overview

From the towering skylines of Mumbai to the expanding industrial hubs of Odisha and Andhra Pradesh, cement is the invisible force cementing India's growth story: strong, essential, and transformative. As India races towards becoming the world's third-largest economy with a projected GDP of $7.3 trillion by 2030, cement demand will surge across housing, infrastructure, and industrial sectors.

Production Capacity
